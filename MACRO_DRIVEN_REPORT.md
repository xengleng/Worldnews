# Macro-Driven Report Structure

## ✅ **New Report Format Implemented**

Following your feedback, the report now follows a **top-down macro-driven approach**:

## 📊 **Report Flow:**
```
1. MACRO OVERVIEW (Global drivers)
   ↓
2. SECTOR ANALYSIS (By asset class)  
   ↓
3. INDIVIDUAL ASSETS (Specific tickers)
   ↓
4. ACTION PLAN (Clear recommendations)
   ↓
5. RISK ASSESSMENT (Key risks & mitigation)
```

## 🌍 **SECTION 1: MACRO OVERVIEW**
**What's included:**
- **Monetary Policy:** Fed rate expectations (dovish/hawkish)
- **Commodities & Geopolitics:** Oil prices, drivers (wars/policies)
- **Global Forex:** USD/SGD dynamics, rate differentials
- **Macro Drivers:** Clear "why" behind market moves

## 📈 **SECTION 2: SECTOR ANALYSIS** 
**Sectors Covered:**
1. **GOLD** - Safe haven, inflation hedge
2. **TECH** - Growth, AI thematic  
3. **SINGAPORE** - Defensive, dividend focus
4. **FOREX** - Rate differential plays

**Each sector includes:**
- **Outlook:** BULLISH/NEUTRAL/BEARISH
- **Why:** Fundamental reasoning
- **Catalyst:** What could drive movement
- **Risk:** Key concerns
- **Action:** Sector-level recommendation

## 🎯 **SECTION 3: INDIVIDUAL ASSETS**
**For each of 12 tracked symbols:**
- **Latest News:** Most recent relevant article
- **My View:** Expert opinion on the asset
- **Why Track:** Investment thesis
- **Current Driver:** What's moving it now
- **Action:** Specific BUY/HOLD/SELL recommendation

## 🚀 **SECTION 4: ACTION PLAN**
**Clear, actionable recommendations:**
- **Primary Bias:** RISK-ON/RISK-OFF based on macro
- **5-Point Action Plan:** Specific steps for each asset class
- **Key Monitors:** What to watch for changes

## ⚠️ **SECTION 5: RISK ASSESSMENT**
**Top 5 Risks:**
1. Fed policy error
2. Geopolitical escalation  
3. China slowdown
4. Tech valuation excess
5. USD strength

**Risk Mitigation:** Practical steps to manage risks
